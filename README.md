## 💡 Best Practices for Data Recovery
To increase your chances of success, follow these guidelines:

*   **Stop Using Your iPhone:** As soon as you realize data is missing, stop taking new photos or downloading new apps. This prevents the phone from overwriting the deleted data.
*   **Disable Airplane Mode:** Keep your Wi-Fi or cellular connection active for iCloud recovery, but keep the device idle when searching for data.
*   **Use Original Cables:** A faulty cable can cause connection drops during a scan. Use only official Apple cables or certified high-quality alternatives.
*   **Keep Your Computer Close:** Ensure your laptop or desktop power settings do not put the machine to sleep during the scanning process.
*   **Update iTunes:** Always use the latest version of iTunes available from the official Microsoft Store or Apple website to ensure compatibility with newer iOS versions.
